# Character: Defect
 
## Unique mechanic: Orbs
Lightning (passive damage + evoke burst), Frost (passive block + evoke block), Dark (grows each turn + evoke
massive damage), Plasma (passive energy + evoke energy).
 
## Core Strength
Defect scales through Focus — each point of Focus increases ALL orb passive and evoke
values. A Frost orb with 0 Focus blocks 2/turn; with 5 Focus it blocks 7/turn. This
multiplicative relationship means Focus is the single most important stat. Without it,
orbs are trivial. With it, they are overwhelming.
 
## Key Synergies
A. Focus burst / debuff-cleanse
- Core: Biased Cognition
- Enablers: Core Surge, Orange Pellets
- Commit when: orb generation already exists
- Trap: taking Biased without cleanse/artifact or without enough orb output
- Heuristic: Biased is premium only if the deck can exploit short-term huge Focus or negate the debuff

B. Frost / Focus control
- Core: Defragment, Glacier
- Enablers: Coolheaded, Loop, Capacitor, Frozen Core
- Commit when: Frost already meaningfully reduces incoming damage
- Trap: stacking passive scaling without enough draw/orb generation
- Heuristic: this is the default stable shell; survive first, then add a finisher

C. Consume / orb-slot scaling
- Core: Consume
- Enablers: Inserter, Capacitor, Runic Capacitor
- Commit when: current orbs are already high quality
- Trap: taking Consume too early with only 3 slots and weak orb generation
- Heuristic: convert excess slots into Focus only after the orb shell exists

D. Dark finisher
- Core: Dark orb generation, Loop
- Enablers: Recursion, Dualcast, Multi-Cast, stall
- Commit when: you can reliably stall and choose the evoke timing
- Trap: Darkness without evoke/control
- Heuristic: Dark is a payout engine, not a shell

E. Lightning tempo / AoE
- Core: Electrodynamics
- Enablers: Lightning generation, Thunder Strike
- Commit when: multi-enemy fights are a problem or Lightning density is already high
- Trap: Thunder Strike without enough Lightning channeled
- Heuristic: Electrodynamics solves AoE; Thunder Strike is a payoff, not a starter

F. Zero-cost recursion
- Core: All for One
- Enablers: Beam Cell, Go for the Eyes, FTL, Claw, Hologram, Recycle, Scrape
- Commit when: 0-cost density + draw are both real
- Trap: forcing Claw, or letting Scrape discard critical pieces
- Heuristic: value discard-pile setup and redraw speed more than raw card count

G. Power-spam
- Core: Heatsinks, Storm, Creative AI
- Enablers: Mummified Hand, Echo Form, sustain/survival
- Commit when: setup turns are survivable or Mummified Hand is present
- Trap: greedy power stacking in fast fights
- Heuristic: turn setup into draw/output, not dead tempo

H. Energy-cheat / big-spell
- Core: Meteor Strike
- Enablers: Double Energy, TURBO, energy relics, Fission, Recycle, draw
- Commit when: high-cost cards can actually be cast consistently
- Trap: expensive payoffs without energy support
- Heuristic: build one explosive resource-conversion turn, then snowball

I. Reprogram / no-focus off-axis
- Core: Reprogram
- Enablers: Core Surge, Orange Pellets, low-orb-dependence attack/block shell
- Commit when: the deck is no longer relying on Focus/orbs as main scaling
- Trap: mixing Reprogram with Frost/Focus shell
- Heuristic: this is a branch away from the orb game, not a supplement to it
 
## Key Cards
Frontload: Ball Lightning (damage + channel Lightning), Cold Snap (damage + channel
  Frost), Compile Driver (damage + draw per orb), Sunder
AoE: Electrodynamics (makes all Lightning AoE — game-changing), Blizzard, Doom and Gloom
Block: Glacier (best block card — 8 block + 2 Frost), Chill, Coolheaded, Genetic
  Algorithm (block grows each combat), Self Repair (heal 7 at end of combat)
Scaling: Defragment (always take), Biased Cognition (almost always take — play early),
  Echo Form (almost always take), Creative AI, Capacitor
Draw: Coolheaded, Skim, Compile Driver, Machine Learning, Hologram
Utility: Seek (tutor any card from draw pile — incredible), Buffer (prevent next HP
  loss instance), Equilibrium (retain entire hand)
 
## Common Mistakes
- Not prioritizing Focus. Orbs without Focus do trivial amounts. Defragment should be
  taken almost always on sight.
- Channeling orbs without enough slots. Default 3 slots fill fast. Capacitor or Inserter
  needed for orb-heavy builds.
- Playing Biased Cognition too late. The -1 Focus/turn means it's strongest played Turn
  1-2. Waiting until Turn 5 gives only 2-3 good turns before Focus goes negative.
  Artifact can cancel the decay — Orange Pellets or Artifact Potion pairs perfectly.
- Ignoring Frost. Lightning is flashy but Frost keeps you alive. A Frost-focused build
  with Focus is one of the most consistent strategies in the game.
- Skipping Echo Form because of the 3-cost. The doubling effect is worth one slow turn.
 
## Act-Specific Notes
Act 1: Starter orbs are weak without Focus. Prioritize Ball Lightning and Cold Snap
  as frontload that also channels orbs. Compile Driver is strong early draw. Take
  Defragment on sight.
Act 2: You need Frost + Focus online or you will get overwhelmed by multi-enemy fights.
  Glacier is the best single card for stabilizing. Electrodynamics solves AoE.
  Biased Cognition is usually worth taking even without Artifact.
Act 3: Keep removing un-synergized cards to make your deck more effiecient.
Echo Form wins boss fights if found. Against Awakened One, front-load powers
  Turn 1 or pivot to orb damage without new powers mid-fight. Frost + high Focus
  makes you nearly invincible against everything else.
